History

Our Company: Started as a family business, Cook Door became now part of the group that belongs to the holding company: "International company for food industries" (ICFI), a share holding Egyptian company that owns the following companies as well:
  • Cook Door
  • Golden bakery
  • Customer voice

Our Vision

from a small restaurant in the Egyptian suburbs, to one of the best fast food restaurants in Egypt and the MENA region. Our vision is to become the 1# quick service food chain when it comes to taste and service beyond customer expectations and expand worldwide through a professional management and franchising system.
